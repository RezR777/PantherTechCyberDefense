Vulnerability Assessment & Management

Continuous discovery, prioritized remediation, and verification.

What we do: Asset discovery, authenticated & unauthenticated scanning, dependency & container scanning, manual verification by engineers, and enterprise reporting with risk scoring (CVSS + contextual business impact).

  • Weekly automated scans with monthly manual validation
  • Third-party / open-source dependency analysis
  • Prioritized remediation roadmap and validation testing

Deliverables: Executive summary, prioritized findings, PoC (when safe), and remediation verification.

Penetration Testing (Web, Mobile, Network)

Realistic adversary simulations to reveal exploitable weaknesses.

Our pentests blend automated discovery with manual exploitation and post-exploitation analysis. We follow a strict Rules of Engagement and provide a remediation-focused report.

Phases:

  1. Scoping & Authorization
  2. Reconnaissance & Enumeration
  3. Exploit & Validate
  4. Post-Exploit Analysis & Reporting

Optional addons: Red team exercises, social-engineering campaigns, phishing simulations.

Managed Detection & Response (MDR)

24/7 detection, triage, and response from our SOC.

We deploy sensors, integrate logs (SIEM/ELK), and run advanced behavioral detection. Our analysts investigate alerts and either escalate to you or execute pre-authorized containment playbooks.

  • Real-time alerting & analyst triage
  • Threat hunting & proactive detection
  • Playbook-driven containment and eradication

Cloud Security & DevSecOps

Secure cloud posture, IaC scanning, and pipeline integration.

We harden cloud accounts (IAM least privilege, secure networking, encryption at rest/in transit) and embed security into CI/CD pipelines using SCA/SAST/DAST tools.

Features: Infrastructure as Code review, runtime drift detection, container image scanning, cloud cost & risk optimization.

Incident Response & Forensics

Rapid containment, evidence collection, and recovery guidance.

When things go wrong we provide rapid-response teams that perform triage, containment, root-cause analysis, and forensic evidence preservation for legal/regulatory needs.

  • Remote or onsite containment
  • Malware analysis and IOC creation
  • Post-incident report and lessons learned

Security Awareness & Training

Practical programs to reduce human risk.

Tailored training modules, phishing campaigns, and role-based workshops (devs, admins, execs). We provide measurable KPIs and progress reports.

Compliance, Audit & Advisory
